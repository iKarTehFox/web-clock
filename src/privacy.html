Data Collection

I, Diego P. (iKarTehFox), do not collect any data whatsoever from visitors of the Website. Your privacy is important, and no personal information or usage data is gathered, stored, or shared by me.

Weather Widget

The Website includes a feature that allows you to use the OpenWeatherMap widget to obtain the current weather for your location or any location you set. Please note the following:

GPS: If you choose to use the GPS feature, your device's geolocation data will be used to determine your location. This geolocation data is not sent to or stored by me. Instead, it is used solely with the OpenWeatherMap API to fetch weather data.

OpenWeatherMap API: The use of your location data is governed by the privacy policy and terms of OpenWeatherMap. For more information on how they handle your data, please review their Privacy Policy, including their Terms.
